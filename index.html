How the Global Raffle Works

A simple, transparent structure designed to connect participants across markets with BYD’s new-energy vision.

1. Choose your ticket tier

Select Standard, Premium or Elite depending on the level of participation and visibility you prefer in the global draw cycle.

2. Confirm your entry

Secure your entry through the official BYD communication channels. Each validated entry receives a unique raffle reference.

3. Global draw and announcement

Eligible entries are pooled for the official Annual End-of-Year draw. Successful participants are notified directly and through authorised BYD channels.
